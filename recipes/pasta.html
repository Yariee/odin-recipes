This pasta recipe can be too hot to handle at times!

This pasta is hit or miss on spiciness for myself. Sometimes it's too hot, sometimes it's not hot enought!

Saying that, I LOVE when my girlfriend (Ana) makes this recipe for us.

Here are the ingredients that are needed to make cajun pasta!

  • Pasta (We prefer Penne Rigate)
  • H-E-B Cajun Sauce
  • Sasuage
  • Shrimp
  • Water to boil
  • Pot for boiling the water

Here are the steps involved in making Cajun Pasta!

  1. Bring the water in the pot to a boil
  2. Once the water is boiling, insert the pasta into the pot to cook itself. We typically leave the pasta in for 10 mins.
  3. Once the pasta is cooked, empty the water into a nearby sink and pour the H-E-B Cajun sauce onto the pasta itself.
  4. Let the pasta cook further with the shrimp and sasuage in the pot!
  5. Pour the contents onto a plater after 10 mins and enjoy!

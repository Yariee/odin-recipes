This is THE recipe that is used in the Ana and Yair household!

This recipe goes back generations, passed down from my kitties that love to eat my bread as I prepare my sandwiches!

These are the ingredients that are needed to make the perfect sandwich that is kitty approved:

  • Bread (I prefer to use Dave's Killer Bread)
  • Turkey
  • Sharp Cheddar
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayo
  • Honey Mustard

Once these ingredients are obtained, we can start making the kitty approved sandwich! The steps are as follows:

  1. Take two slice of your prefered bread and spread both mayo and honey mustard on them
  2. As you put the bread to the side, make sure to cut slices of lettuce to be put on the sandwich itself
  3. Next you want to slice the tomato and put them onto the bread itself as well, I should not that both the lettuce and tomato will only go on one bread slice
  4. Once the lettuce and tomato is on the bread, you can lay the turkey and the sharp cheddar onto the empty bread slice
  5. Put both bread slices together and you have no made the perfect, kitty approved, sandwich!

Who doesn't love a wrap sized pizza that takes 2 minutes to make!

Hot pockets are a go-to meal in my household. Although I try not to take myself too seriously, hot pockets are wrap sized pizzas that are easily made in 2 minutes!

The following Ingredients are needed to make hot pockets:

  • Hot pocket of any flavor, and that's it!

The following steps need to be followed to make a perfect hot pocket:

  1. In my household, hot pockets are not removed from the plastic package. Instead we heat the hotpocket with the plastic wrapper! Heat the hot pocket with a microwavable plate for 2 minutes.
  2. Once the 2 minutes are up, let the hot pocket sit for another 2 minutes to cool down.
  3. Take the hot pocket out of the plastic wrapper and enjoy your bite sized pizza!

Sandwiches by Yair!

Sandwiches are great, easy and simple meals that can be during picnics and even for dinner.

Everyone's version of a sandwich is different, although, we make ours with different sets of ingredients.

Ingredients

The Ingredients below are used to a Yair sandwich that can be enjoyed during lunch or dinner time!

  • Bread, I prefer to use wheat bread
  • Turkey
  • Any type of cheese, I prefer to use colby jack
  • Mayo
  • Honey Mustard
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Tomatoes

Steps to make a sandwich

Here are the steps needed to make a Yair Sandwich

  1. First we want to cut the romain lettuce into equal slices
  2. Next, we want to take spread mayo and honey mustard onto both slices of bread
  3. After we spread both mayo and honey mustard onto our bread, we want to start adding the Turkey and Cheese slices
  4. Once the turkey and cheese is added, we can put it to the side and start slicing our tomatoes into equal pieces as well
  5. As soon as all ingredients are cut, we can add the romain lettuce and tomatoes onto our turkey and cheese and we have finally made the perfect sandwich!

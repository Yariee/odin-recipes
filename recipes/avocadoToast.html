Avocado Toast!

Avocado toast is a great breakfast, lunch or even dinner that is cheap and delicious!

My girlfriend and I love to eat it almost daily and it's very fast and easy to make!

Ingredients

The Ingredients below are needed to make the best avocado toast known to man!

  • Bread of your choise, we prefer wheat bread
  • Avocados, obviously
  • Everything Bagel seasoning
  • Bacon
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Your choice of hot sauce, we prefer to use Cholulua

Steps to make Avocado Toast

  1. Add avocado oil, or any cooking oil of your choosing to a pan and heat to medium heat.
  2. Once the pan is hot enough, cook eggs to your liking. I personally like my eggs scrambled!
  3. In another pan, heat pan to medium heat and add bacon. Make sure to cook well enough to eat so you wont be sick!
  4. Once eggs are cooked, remove from pan and season with salt, pepper and everything bagel seasoning. Bacon can be removed if cooked at this time as well.
  5. While the eggs and bacon are cooling down, begin to mash up the avocados in a small bowl. Make sure to add everything bagel seasoning for extra flavor!
  6. Once the avocado's are mashed, add the eggs and bacon on top of one slice of bread.
  7. Add avocado on top and voila, you have just made the best avocado toast the universe has ever known!

Cereal, my go-to breakfast, lunch and dinner

My most controversial pick yet, CEREAL

I without a doubt, LOVE CEREAL

I know anyone reading this won't think cereal is a real recipes, and I have no problem with your opinion

But if it wasn't for my girlfriend, I would only eat cereal so this is my bread and butter meal for any day of the week

Ingredients

  • Your favorite kind of cereal, I am a old man and love Special K with Yogurt and Vanilla granola
  • 2% milk, Oat milk is also used in my household

Steps to make the best cereal

  1. First, and I do mean FIRST we pour the cereal amount we would like
  2. Next take your favorite milk and pour just the right amount where the milk doesn't overfill the cereal
  3. Finally, enjoy a fresh bowl of delicious cereal!

Cereal Recipe!

Cereal is something that I can eat all day, everyday since I wad a kid! This simple recipe shows you the correct way to make a bowl of cereal!

Ingredients needed for our recipe:

  • Milk
  • Cereal of your choice, although Fruity Pebbles is best!

The following steps are needed in order to make the perfect bowl of cereal:

  1. Have a bowl ready for your cereal, as well as I spoon. We're going to first POUR THE CEREAL into the bowl and not the other way around like some kind of pyscho person!
  2. Next we will pour the milk over the cereal. We need to make sure there is just enough milk so that we can still see the ceral sit on top of the our milk.
  3. And wallah! We have just made the perfect bowl of cereal, courtesy of this recipe!

Mama mia we love some lasagna!

Lasagna is a great family meal that can take lots of time to perfect but will be worth it in the end!

The following Ingredients are needed to cook the best lasagna on planet earth:

  • Meat, preferably Italian sasauge and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce, and 2 cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar
  • Fresh parsely, basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper.
  • Store bought or homemade lasanga noodle.
  • Cheeses such as Parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese!
  • Egg to help bind the ricotta to the noodles.

The following steps are needed to make the lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture to help bind to the noodles
  4. Layer the noodles
  5. Cover the mixture with foil and bake for 1 hour
  6. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before serving!
